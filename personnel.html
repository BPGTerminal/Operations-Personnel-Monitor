YOUR SETTINGS CSV URL
This loads your event settings (team names, colors, map location) from your Google Sheet.

It looks like:
https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/e/.../pub?...&output=csv
📋 From Google Sheets → File → Share → Publish to web → CSV
